About Margaret

Creative Fashion Designer & Founder of STYLICHE

Creative and passionate fashion designer with a Certificate in Fashion Design and over four years of hands-on experience in garment construction, sewing, styling, and design. Known for blending contemporary trends with rich cultural influences.

As the founder of STYLICHE, I've built a brand recognized for bold, stylish, and expressive fashion. My expertise lies in translating cultural aesthetics into modern fashion while maintaining the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship.

My passion extends beyond design to include acting and modeling, allowing me to bring a unique perspective to fashion that combines performance, visual storytelling, and cultural expression.
