About Margaret

Fashion Designer • Stylist • Creative Visionary

My Story

I am Margaret Saddick, a creative and passionate fashion designer with over four years of hands-on experience in the fashion industry.

My journey began with a deep passion for sewing, acting, and modeling. These creative outlets have allowed me to express myself and connect with others in unique ways. Sewing lets me create beautiful and practical items, acting gives me a chance to explore different characters and stories, and modeling allows me to collaborate with talented photographers and designers.

With a Certificate in Fashion Design from Vidols School of Fashion and Design, I specialize in blending contemporary trends with rich cultural influences. As the founder of STYLICHE, I've built a brand recognized for bold, stylish, and expressive fashion.

My expertise extends beyond design to include advanced garment construction, personal styling, and creative direction. I believe in translating cultural aesthetics into modern fashion while maintaining the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship.
